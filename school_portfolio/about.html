🏫 About Shemrock Thakurpukur

Welcome to Shemrock Thakurpukur – Where Learning Begins with Joy!

At Shemrock Thakurpukur, we believe that early childhood is the most important phase in a child’s life. It is during these foundational years that children begin to form their sense of identity, values, curiosity, and emotional well-being. That's why we’ve created a school that blends love, learning, and laughter every single day.

As a proud member of the prestigious Shemrock chain of preschools, with over 30 years of early education excellence, we bring a trusted legacy of nurturing young minds into confident learners — right here in the heart of Thakurpukur, Kolkata.

Our preschool offers a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where children feel loved, respected, and encouraged to grow. With colorful classrooms, interactive learning tools, and a play-based curriculum, we focus on developing your child’s intellectual, emotional, social, and physical skills holistically.

✨ What Makes Us Special

  • Child-Centered Approach: Every activity is designed keeping in mind the natural learning style of children.
  • Experienced & Caring Faculty: Our dedicated teachers guide and support each child with patience and love.
  • Safe & Hygienic Environment: We prioritize child safety with secure premises and high hygiene standards.
  • Play-Based Learning: We make learning fun through stories, games, music, dance, and hands-on activities.
  • Regular Parent Engagement: We keep parents informed and involved in their child’s early learning journey.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide a joyful, secure, and enriching environment where children can grow into confident, responsible, and curious learners. Our mission is to lay a strong foundation not only for academic success but also for emotional intelligence, empathy, and resilience.

👁️ Our Vision

To be Thakurpukur’s most trusted and loved preschool, empowering every child to unlock their full potential in a space that celebrates their uniqueness. We envision a generation of lifelong learners who are curious, kind, and courageous.
